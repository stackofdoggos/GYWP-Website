Field Notes: When the Game Outgrows the Town

In several counties, the official boundary of play now exceeds the official boundary of the county. Commissioners have responded by declaring the concept of "out of bounds" unconstitutional.

Overlap zones, the places where three or more fields share the same parking lot, have become the most heavily trafficked real estate on the continent. Realtors in Atlanta list "on the forty-yard line" as a selling point. Realtors in Nebraska list "not on the forty-yard line" as a selling point.
